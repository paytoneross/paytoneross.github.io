Yard Wars

       This project was my first ever experience with local multiplayer in Unity. In a group of 3 we put together a 2-4 player experience where players must fight to keep trash out of their yard, and instead in their neighbors. My role on the team was Gameplay Director. I was responsible for coding the multiplayer mechanics, such as assigning controls and instantiating player characters. I was also in charge of the player movement code, and I programmed the dog AI using state machines.

       We had to implement AI into our game somehow, so I had the idea that a dog that runs around and poops, adding trash to whoevers yard he's in, would be a fun gameplay mechanic. There were challenges with the multiplayer mechanics that I had to overcome, such as making sure the correct controllers get assigned to the correct players, and figuring out the spawning of the players once controls had been assigned. Overall I gained a lot of experience in Unity and I am very happy with how much fun the game ended up being.
