Escape The Tomb

       This was the first project that I worked on in the program. I was just starting working in Unity and in Animate, as you can see from my state of the art animation and shading. We had to chose one of three 2D stub games, a platformer, tower defense, or top down shooter, and make our own game out of them. I chose the platformer, and I decided to create a game that combines platforming challenges with metroidvania-like exploration and progression. The player explores the interior of a scary haunted pyramid, and has find movement upgrades in order to survive and escape.

       My initial idea for the game was much grander than I ended up being able to make, but I'm still proud of what I came up with. The biggest things I took from this game were learning how to work in sprints, avoiding out-scoping myself, and what makes a small game punch above its weight class. When I look back on this game now, all that I see is everything that is wrong with it and how much better I could do it now, which I think that shows how far I've come.
