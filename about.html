About Me

       I am currently a sophmore/junior at Boise State University studying Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile Technologies (GIMM). I say sophmore/junior because I am fast tracking through the program and I am anticipating graduation in Spring 2026 after 3 years of schooling. I am very excited to have found a career where I love what I do and I am eager to contribute something meaningful to the industry. My goal is to become a gameplay software developer, or game level designer, and hopefully someday make my way up to being a creative director on a AAA game project.

       Outside from school, I work as a Phlebotomist. I perform outpatient blood draws on patients of all age ranges, and coordinate with MLS and lab staff on processing, transport, and resulting of medical lab tests. I used to be a nursing major before I switched to GIMM, and in my time in nursing I found my way over to the lab. I am still very interested in healthcare and science, and I love being a part of such a great team. Not many people like getting their blood drawn, and I try as hard as I can to make it a pleasant, quick, and painless experience.

       In my free time I enjoy spending time with my friends and family, playing music, video games, snowboarding, skateboarding, wakeboarding, any type of boarding really. I play a few instruments, and I love listening to music. The way that music makes me feel is one of my biggest inspirations in making games, I want to be able to create experiences that can instill those kinds of feelings in others. Below you will find a list of some of my favorite games, shows, and musicians. The most recent game I have played at this point is The Legend of Zelda: Echoes of Wisdom, and I absolutely loved it.
