About Me

       I am currently a senior at Boise State University studying Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile Technologies (GIMM). I am expecting to graduate in May 2026, and I feel very excited and lucky to have found a career where I love what I do. My goal is to become a gameplay engineer or level designer, and hopefully someday work my way up to being a creative director. I love how games are a conglomeration of so many different art forms that all communicate to the player individually, but can be aligned to create an experience greater than just the sum of its parts.

       Outside of school, I work part time as a Phlebotomist. I perform outpatient blood draws on patients of all age ranges, and coordinate with medical lab scientists on processing and resulting of lab tests.

       In my free time I enjoy hanging out with my friends and family, playing video games, snowboarding, skateboarding, wakeboarding, any type of boarding really. I also play a few instruments, and I love writing and listening to music. The way that music makes me feel is one of my biggest inspirations in making games, I want to create experiences that can instill those kinds of feelings in people.
