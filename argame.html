AR Tower Attack

       This was the second game I developed in my first semester, and the first group game project I worked on in the program. The assignment was to make an AR game experience that could be played on a mobile device. After a few brainstorming sessions, we decided on an FPS where you would spawn a castle using plane detection and once you do a bunch of tiny guys start shooting at you. The goal is to clear them all out while dodging their shots by moving around. My contribution to this project was assisting with AR implementation, the design of the castle, the shooting mechanics, touch screen controls, and UI/UX and HUD design.

       We only had a few weeks to put this project together, and I'm very proud of what we made and how much we learned in such a short amount of time. My biggest takeaways from this project was the value of working on projects as a team. They say teams are stronger than heroes, and that was proven to me when we worked on this project.
