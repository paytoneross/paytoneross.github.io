Welcome to my portfolio website!

My name is Payton Ross, I am a student studying games, interactive media, and mobile technologies at Boise State University. I am immensely passionate about game design, and I aim to create unique and powerful experiences that leave lasting impressions. Alongside my passion for game design, I also have strong working knowledge in various forms of software development, such as app development and web development. I created this page myself to be a showcase of some of the projects I have worked on, as well as a brief overview of my skills and background. I strive to achieve thoughtful and intuitive design across all aspects of my work, and I hope that comes across in my projects showcased here.
