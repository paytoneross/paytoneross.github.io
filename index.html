My Projects

Escape The Tomb

Single player 2D platformer game. This was my first game project and my introduction to Unity and C#. I created this game individually over the course of 6 weeks, and designed all aspects of it.

AR Tower Attack

Single player AR FPS game. In my first group project with a team of 5, my role was gameplay designer. In just 3 weeks we created an FPS game played in real space using advanced plane detection.

Interactive Comic

Interactive comic book experience with a mini auto runner game. In a team of 5, we were tasked with creating an experience that explains a complex quantum science principle. My role was gameplay designer.

Boss Fight Mini Game

Single player FPS boss fight mini game. I created this by myself over the course of a 4 day weekend. The game features fast paced FPS gameplay alongside complex puzzle mechanics.

Yard Wars

2-4 Player local multiplayer game. With a team of 3, we created a game where the players fight to keep junk out of their yard. My role was gameplay designer and AI state machine programmer.

Critter

Single player roguelike game. You play as a mouse exploring a house to find cheese, while trying to avoid the hungry housecat. Collecting cheese allows you to gain powerful abilities that allow you to explore farther.
